About

The beginning of an era.

  • 2009-2011

    Our Humble Beginnings

    We began by having a mission of catching pesky mice. They are always eating leftover food that crumbles onto your floor. Issue is...what if you were saving that for later! How would you then feel?

  • March 2011

    An Agency is Born

    After vehemently combating congress in court, we were able to win and allow us pass a bill to make it illegal to take food that falls on the floor.

  • December 2012

    Transition to Full Service

    Introducing KarKats. The best delivery service out there to pick up mice who pick up your food that you would have otherwise never picked up!

  • July 2014

    Phase Two Expansion

    Forget the mice, there are far more important things. Cats unite nationwide in order to brainstorm how to dominate the earth..

  • September 2019

    Phase Three Expansion

    Cats expand to final form, taking over couches, chairs, tables, sinks, and soon cars, trains, buses, and more...
